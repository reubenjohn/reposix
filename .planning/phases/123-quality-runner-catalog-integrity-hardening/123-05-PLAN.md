|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|

| T-123-11 | Tampering | Two writers targeting DIFFERENT catalog files unnecessarily serialize on one global lock file | accept | acceptable cost for correctness-first simplicity; `--persist` runs are infrequent (phase-close / verifier-mint only) and short-lived, so cross-file contention is not expected to be a real bottleneck; a per-file lock is a legitimate future refinement, not required by SC3's wording ("a single locked persist lane" is explicitly one of the two sanctioned designs) |
