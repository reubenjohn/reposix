DP-2 verdict: GREEN on the true claim (real git-2.43 single-backend push exits 0
after the fix, RED->GREEN in-container). The intake's stateless-connect/`fallback`
mechanism was a misdiagnosis; neither git 2.43 nor 2.54 probes stateless-connect for
the push. Real fix: helper answers `option object-format {<empty>|true|sha1}` -> `ok`
(main.rs). The `fallback` sentinel (stateless_connect.rs) is also fixed for
git-remote-helpers(7) spec-compliance but is not the push-unblocker.
